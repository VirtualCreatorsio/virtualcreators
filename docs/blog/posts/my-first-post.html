This is the first post on the VirtualCreators blog. We wanted a place to share clear, useful content about web design and development: what things cost, when to redesign, and how we work with clients. If you are planning a website or curious how we think about projects, you are in the right place.

We will keep the tone straightforward. No hype. Just practical posts that help you decide and plan. You can also have a look at our work and services if you want to see what we build.

Why This Blog

We started the VirtualCreators blog because we often get the same questions from clients and prospects: how much does a custom site cost, when should we redesign, and is a template enough? Rather than repeat the same answers in every call, we put them into posts. That way you can read at your own pace and come to a call with clearer questions.

The blog is also a way to show how we think. We care about clean design, fast sites, and projects that stay on budget and on time. What we write here reflects that.

What We Do at VirtualCreators

Virtual Creators is a small studio. We design and build websites for businesses that want something that looks good, works well, and fits their goals. That can be a simple brochure site, a bigger content site, or something with e-commerce or custom functionality. We also help with redesigns when an existing site no longer fits.

We work with a clear process: we agree on scope, design and build, then hand over. We have written about custom website cost and custom vs template in other posts if you want more detail.

Tip: skim the blog index

New here? Open the blog index and skim the titles. Pick one or two posts that match what you are thinking about (cost, redesign, or template vs custom). That will give you a quick sense of how we work and what to expect.

Welcome Post FAQ

What is the VirtualCreators blog?

The VirtualCreators blog is where we share practical posts about web design, custom websites, and what goes into building and running sites for businesses. We cover topics like cost, when to redesign, and how we work so readers can plan their own projects better.

Who writes the VirtualCreators blog?

Kjell de Ruiter writes the blog. He runs Virtual Creators and works as a UX/UI designer and web developer. Posts reflect his experience from real client projects and day-to-day work.

What topics does the VirtualCreators blog cover?

We cover web design and development topics that help business owners and decision makers: custom website cost, when to choose a template vs a custom build, when it is time for a redesign, and how to plan and budget. Posts are aimed at being useful, not salesy.

How often does VirtualCreators publish new posts?

We publish when we have something useful to add. There is no fixed schedule. New posts are added to the blog index so you can see the latest there.
